How To Buy

Inu lovers, Assemble!

1. Download Trust Wallet
Simply visit trustwallet.com and download the application. The app is secure and widely used in the DeFi market. (Remember to never share your seed phrase)
 
2. Load your wallet with BNB!

Purchase BNB to fund your wallet. BNB can be bought from Binance or Trust Wallet itself. If you buy from Binance, you need to transfer the BNB to Trust Wallet after purchase.

3. Go the the DApps tab and click on Pancake Swap

Pancake Swap is an exchange that allows users to swap BNB with defi coins. Click ‘Select A Currency’ and enter the contract:

Don’t forget to set your slippage to 12%.
